KYA NAYA HAI
------------
1) Ek alag file judi hai:  plugins/xhamster_engine.py
   - Ye xHamster ka APNA engine hai (yt-dlp se azaad).
   - SAARE xHamster domains/mirrors pehchaanta hai
     (xhamster.com / .desi / .one / .tv, xhamster46.desi,
      m.xhamster.com, xhday, xhvid, xhms.pro, xhopen, etc.)
   - Khud page padhta hai, HLS (.m3u8) nikalta hai, h264+audio
     wali har quality (144p..1080p) banata hai. videoModel
     error / "Wrong key" / silent-av1 ki dikkat khatam.

2) Bot ka flow:
   - xHamster link aate hi PEHLE ye apna engine chalta hai
     -> quality buttons (144p/240p/480p/720p/1080p) + MP3
   - Button dabate hi bot us quality ka .m3u8 yt-dlp+ffmpeg se
     download kar ke ek normal MP4 bana ke bhej deta hai.
   - Agar engine kabhi fail ho -> bot APNE AAP yt-dlp pe
     fallback kar leta hai (purana tarika). Koi crash nahi.

3) BAAKI SAARI SITES bilkul pehle jaisi chalti hain.
   YouTube/Insta/FB/etc. ka kuch nahi badla.
